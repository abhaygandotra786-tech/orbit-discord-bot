Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

This policy explains what data Orbit collects and how it's used.

1. Data we collect

  • Discord account data: your Discord user ID and username (to identify you and display your name).
  • Profile data: information you choose to add — name, age, location, bio, skills, profession, interests, links and category.
  • Activity data: likes, matches, profile views and daily usage counts to power features and limits.
  • Payment data: handled entirely by our payment provider (Dodo Payments). We never see or store your card details — only your subscription status.

2. How we use it

To operate the Service: show profiles, enable search and matching, apply membership benefits, and prevent abuse.

3. What we share

Your profile name and Discord username are visible to other members within the Service. We do not sell your data. We share the minimum necessary with our payment provider to process subscriptions.

4. Data retention & deletion

Your profile is stored until you delete it (/profile delete) or request removal. Deleting your profile removes your profile data from our database.

5. Security

We use encrypted connections and store credentials securely. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

6. Your rights

You may access or delete your data at any time. For requests, contact us via the support server.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted here.
