FAQ

Good questions, quick answers

Is Orbit free?
Yes. Orbit is free to use. You can unlock extra perks by voting or referring friends, and optional paid tiers exist, but the core matching is free.
How does matching work?
You fill out a short profile. Every week Orbit looks at interests, goals and activity, then drops a few curated matches. No endless swiping, just a handful of people worth talking to.
Is it safe?
Yes. Verification, reporting and blocking are free for everyone, always. Orbit uses active moderation and referral vouching to keep the community real.
How do I add Orbit to my server?
Click "Add Orbit to your server", pick your server, and approve. You will be matching members within minutes.
